## Tips for Posting

1. **Frequency**: Post 1-2 times daily, varying between educational, social proof, and CTA posts
2. **Best times**: 9-11am ET and 1-3pm ET for B2B audience
3. **Mix it up**: Don't post only promotional content - 80% value, 20% CTA
4. **Engage**: Reply to comments within first hour, quote tweet relevant conversations
5. **Hashtags**: Use 2-3 relevant hashtags max per post
6. **Pin**: Pin a high-performing CTA post to your profile
