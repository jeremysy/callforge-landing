The Lead Quality Problem

Your marketing team generates leads. They land in the CRM with a name, email, and maybe a company. Your sales team stares at a list of 500 names and has to pick which ones to call first.

They use gut instinct. They use the "spray and pray" method. They call leads that will never buy while the ready buyers go cold.

What AI Lead Enrichment Actually Does

Here's the system I build:

1. Company Intelligence

Company size, revenue, industry, tech stack, recent funding, job postings, news mentions. All pulled automatically within minutes of lead creation.

2. Contact Intelligence

LinkedIn profile, role tenure, previous companies, content they've published, buying signals from social activity.

3. Intent Scoring

Are they actively researching solutions? Have they visited your pricing page? Downloaded multiple resources? AI scores every lead on buying readiness.

4. Personalized Talking Points

AI generates customized outreach based on their specific situation. "I noticed you recently hired 3 engineers and posted about scaling challenges..."

The Speed Advantage

Research from InsideSales shows that responding to a lead within 5 minutes makes you 21x more likely to qualify them. 21 times.

But how can your rep respond intelligently in 5 minutes if they need 20 minutes to research the company first?

With AI enrichment, the research is done before the lead even hits your rep's queue. They see a lead with full context, a priority score, and suggested talking points. They can make that 5-minute call actually count.
